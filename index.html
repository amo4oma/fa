ABOUT US

FADL ALALIMI FOR TRADING & CONTRACTING is a specialized and market leader in the Republic of Yemen in the fields of design, civil works, cable trenching, electrical works, sewerage network, wastewater treatment basins, electric and solar water pumps, and sewage suction pumps, as well as the supply of construction tools, pipes, electrical tools, heavy equipment, and generators. FADL ALALIMI FOR TRADING & CONTRACTING was founded in 2002 in Sana'a, and in 2008, the firm increased its local market presence by opening a second office in Aden and most recently, in Mukalla, in 2020.

Our Mission

The company's mission is to satisfy our clients with our excellent, high-quality, and sufficient construction services and products, as well as our team's passion and commitment. The firm supplies and implement of water, electricity and green energy projects. And provide the latest technology necessary to complete the work entrusted with accuracy and professionalism.
